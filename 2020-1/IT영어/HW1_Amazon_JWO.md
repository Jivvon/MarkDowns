### summary

 Amazon makes software called "Just Walk Out" that allows shoppers to simply scan a smartphone app when pay for. The software bills the customer and issues a receipt online. An industry expert expects Amazon to make a lot of money from welling the software to other stores. However, a retail expert worries that, "there will be fewer jobs as automation comes in".
